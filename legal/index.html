Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2024

1. Overview

NIS2 Shield ("we", "our") is an open source project. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy explains how we process data when you visit nis2shield.com.

2. Data We Collect

We do not directly collect personal data via this website. However, technical data is processed by our service providers:

  • Hosting (GitHub Pages): May collect IP addresses for security logs.
  • CDNs (Tailwind, Lucide): May process your IP address to deliver assets.
  • Gitpod: If you use the "Live Demo", your interaction is governed by Gitpod's privacy policy.

3. Purpose of Processing

The processing of technical data (IP address) is necessary for the technical delivery of the website (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR - Legitimate Interest).

4. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectification, erasure, and restriction of processing. Contact us at hello@nis2shield.com for any requests.

Cookie Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2024

1. No First-Party Cookies

We do not set any cookies on nis2shield.com. We do not use Google Analytics or other tracking pixels.

2. Third-Party Services

Some external services linked from our site may set cookies when you interact with them:

  • GitHub: If you navigate to our repository or sign in.
  • Gitpod: If you launch the Live Demo environment.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish through your browser settings.
