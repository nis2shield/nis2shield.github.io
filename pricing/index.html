Frequently Asked Questions

Is the code really open source?

Yes, 100%. All core libraries (django-nis2-shield, react-guard, infrastructure) are MIT licensed. You can use, modify, and redistribute freely. Even commercially.

What do I get by paying?

Priority support, faster response times, early access to security patches, and enterprise services like on-site deployment and custom training.

Can hospitals use the free version?

Absolutely. The free version is production-ready and NIS2 compliant. Professional/Enterprise tiers are for organizations that need support SLAs.
